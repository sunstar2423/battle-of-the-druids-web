Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

Information We Collect

Battle of the Druids collects minimal information to provide and improve our gaming experience:

  • Game Data: Character names, game progress, and settings stored locally in your browser
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data via Google Analytics (page visits, game events, device type)
  • Advertising: Google AdSense may collect data for personalized ads

How We Use Information

  • Save your game progress locally on your device
  • Understand how players interact with our game
  • Improve game features and performance
  • Display relevant advertisements

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Game saves are stored in your browser and never sent to our servers
  • Cookies: Used by Google Analytics and AdSense for analytics and advertising
  • No Account Required: We don't collect personal information like emails or passwords

Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense: For advertising (Privacy Policy)

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's local storage to delete game saves
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
